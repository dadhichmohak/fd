College Essentials!

Hey there, College Freshers! ЁЯОУ Are you eagerly counting down the days until you start your new chapter on campus? As you prepare to embark on this exciting journey, it's essential to be well-equipped with all the college essentials you'll need to make your experience comfortable, organized, and fun! ЁЯМЯ

We've put together a comprehensive college packing list to ensure you don't miss a thing when you head off to college. From dorm room essentials to study supplies and everything in between, let's dive right in:

  1. Clothing and Accessories
    • Sufficient everyday outfits, including casual and formal attire (1-2 formal attire)
    • Towels, undergarments, shoes (formal and casual), socks, and sleepwear
    • Belts, handkerchief, napkin
    • Matress, blanket
    • Bedsheet, pillow and pillow cover, curtain
  2. Toiletries and Personal Care Items
    • Toothbrush, toothpaste etc...
    • Soap, shampoo, conditioner, comb, cream and oil
    • Detergent and washing soap
    • Trimmer / Shaving Kit, Nail cutter, scissors
    • Bathroom stuff (bucket, mug etc...)
  3. Electronics and Appliances
    • Laptop, mobile
    • Laptop Accessories (mouse, keyboard, etc...)
    • Pendrive or external hard disk [1]
    • Chargers and other cables
    • Headphones, earphones, tws...
    • Alarm clock (especially for night owls)
    • Extension board (see accordingly)
  4. Study Essentials

    5. You can buy it at the campus also but if you have extra then come with it.

    • Notebooks, files, folders, pens, pencils, highlighters, and marker
    • Stapler and pins, tape, glue
    • Sticky notes
    • Scientific Calculator (maybe branch specific, prefer to buy after consulting with your professors)
  5. Other Items
    • Personal identification documents (Aadhaar card)
    • Basic medicines
    • Hangers, pins (for clothing)
    • Basic utensils
    • Snacks (home made especially)
    • Mosquito repellent cream, machine and liquid
      (Odomos, goodknight, allout)

    Fill the below list acc to you!

  6. Personal Interest
    • Family photo
    • Books you read, want to read (prefer hard-copy than soft-copy)
    • Any hobby related equipment
