IITs

Are you a fresher who has secured admission into one of India's prestigious Indian Institutes of Technology (IITs)? Congratulations on beginning this exciting new chapter of your life! At Freshers Dwar, we understand that joining an IIT is a dream come true for many students, and we're here to help you make the most of your journey.
Our mission is to provide accurate and first-hand information about IITs directly from the students studying there. We believe that the best way to understand a college is through the experiences of its students. On this page, you will find detailed college reviews, accompanied by genuine photos, hostel insights, and room information for selected IITs.
