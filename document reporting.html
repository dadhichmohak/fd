A Comprehensive Guide to Documents Required by Freshers at the
'Time of Reporting'

Congratulations on securing admission into a prestigious institute! As you prepare to embark on your academic journey, it is essential to gather all the necessary documents required during the reporting process. These documents serve as proof of your eligibility, identity, and other essential details. In this blog post, we will provide you with an overview of the documents typically required by freshers during the reporting process.

  • JOSAA Final Allotment Letter (hard copy)
  • JEE Adv Admit Card (for IITs) / JEE Mains Admit Card (for NIT + system)
  • Class X (High School) Board Certificate/Birth Certificate
  • Class XII Board Certificate Mark Sheet
  • Migration Certificate and TC
  • Character Certificate (ask your school)
  • Caste Certificate (ST/SC/OBC-NC) (If Applicable)
  • EWS Certificate
  • Income Certificate (for fee remission if eligible)
Additional Certificates

In addition to the aforementioned documents, there may be other certificates whose formats will be provided by the institute on its official website. These certificates may include medical fitness certificates, conduct certificates, and more. Stay updated with the institute's instructions and submit these certificates as required.
