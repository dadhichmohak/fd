Mission-Vision

  • Strengthen Alumni Bonds: Foster lasting connections and a sense of community among graduates of the institution.
  • Support Lifelong Learning: Facilitate ongoing education and professional development for alumni.
  • Facilitate Mentorship: Enable experienced alumni to mentor and guide current students and recent graduates.
  • Advance Careers: Provide resources and networking opportunities to boost alumni's professional growth.
  • Enhance Institutional Impact: Engage alumni in contributing to the institution's success through various means, including financial support and feedback for improvement.
